**DAY 1: Prayagraj Historical Exploration**

⏰ 8:00 AM - VISIT: Allahabad Fort (⭐ Rating: 4.6)
📍 Address: Allahabad Fort, Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh 211002
🕒 Hours: Sunrise to Sunset
💰 Entry: ₹20 per person (Indians), ₹200 per person (Foreigners)
⏱️ Duration: 2-3 hours
✨ Why Visit: A majestic Mughal fort situated at the confluence of the Ganga, Yamuna, and Saraswati rivers. It holds historical significance, having witnessed many important events throughout Indian history and boasts stunning architecture.

🍽️ 11:00 AM - BREAKFAST: Kashi Ki Rasoi (⭐ Rating: 4.5)
📍 Address: 123/A, Civil Lines, Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh 211001
💰 Price Range: ₹150-300 per person
🍛 Cuisine: Awadhi & Mughlai
⭐ Famous For: Malai Ki Gilori, Bedmi Puri with Aloo Sabzi, Sheermal
🍴 Specialties: Authentic Awadhi flavors, use of local spices and ingredients, traditional cooking methods.


⏰ 1:00 PM - VISIT: Anand Bhavan Museum (⭐ Rating: 4.4)
📍 Address: Swatantrata Sangram Sangrahalaya, Anand Bhavan, Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh 211002
🕒 Hours: 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM (Closed on Mondays)
💰 Entry: ₹50 per person (Indians), ₹200 per person (Foreigners)
⏱️ Duration: 1-2 hours
✨ Why Visit: The ancestral home of the Nehru-Gandhi family, this museum showcases the lives and contributions of Jawaharlal Nehru, Indira Gandhi, and other prominent figures of Indian independence.  It offers a glimpse into India's struggle for freedom.

🍽️ 3:00 PM - LUNCH: Kashi Ki Rasoi (⭐ Rating: 4.5)
📍 Address: 12/A, Civil Lines, Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh 211001
💰 Price Range: ₹300-600 per person
🍛 Cuisine: Awadhi and Mughlai
⭐ Famous For: Galawati Kebabs, Biryani, Nihari Kulcha
🍴 Specialties: Authentic Awadhi spices and preparations, elegant ambiance      

⏰ 5:00 PM - VISIT: Akshayavat (⭐ Rating: 4.2)
📍 Address: Near Allahabad Fort, Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh 211002
🕒 Hours: Open throughout the day
💰 Entry: Free
⏱️ Duration: 30 minutes - 1 hour
✨ Why Visit: A sacred banyan tree believed to be immortal.  It's considered a significant religious site and holds a special place in Hindu mythology and is a tranquil spot for reflection.

🍽️ 7:00 PM - DINNER: Ganga Kinare Bhojanalaya (⭐ Rating: 4.5)
📍 Address: 123, Shivpur, Near Sangam, Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh 211001
💰 Price Range: ₹300-600 per person
🍛 Cuisine: Awadhi & Mughlai
⭐ Famous For: Galawati Kebabs, Sheermal, Biryani, Nihari Kulcha
🍴 Specialties: Authentic Awadhi spices and slow-cooked preparations.  Offers stunning views of the Ganga.

**DAY 2:  Spiritual and Sacred Sites of Prayagraj**

⏰ 8:00 AM - VISIT: Triveni Sangam (⭐ Rating: 4.7)
📍 Address: Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh 211001
🕒 Hours: Open 24 hours
💰 Entry: Free
⏱️ Duration: 1-2 hours
✨ Why Visit: The confluence of three sacred rivers – Ganga, Yamuna, and Saraswati (mythological) – is considered the holiest place for Hindus.  Millions of pilgrims visit during Kumbh Mela.

🍽️ 10:00 AM - BREAKFAST: Sharma Ji Ki Kachori (⭐ Rating: 4.2)
📍 Address: Near Bade Hanuman Mandir, Chowk, Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh
💰 Price Range: ₹50-150 per person
🍛 Cuisine: Street Food
⭐ Famous For: Kachori Sabzi, Jalebi, Samosa, Poori-Sabzi
🍴 Specialties: Generous portions, crispy kachoris, fresh ingredients, quick service, a Prayagraj institution.

⏰ 11:30 AM - VISIT: Akshayavat (⭐ Rating: 4.4)
📍 Address: Near Triveni Sangam, Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh 211001
🕒 Hours: Open 24 hours
💰 Entry: Free
⏱️ Duration: 30 minutes - 1 hour
✨ Why Visit: An ancient banyan tree believed to be immortal, mentioned in Hindu scriptures.  It's a significant religious site and a popular spot for prayers and contemplation.

🍽️ 1:00 PM - LUNCH: Sharma Ji ka Dhaba (⭐ Rating: 4.2)
📍 Address: Near Hanuman Mandir, Chowk, Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh 211002
💰 Price Range: ₹150-300 per person
🍛 Cuisine: North Indian, Street Food
⭐ Famous For: Aloo Paratha, Dal Makhani, Chole Bhature, Kachori Sabzi
🍴 Specialties: Generous portions, home-style cooking, quick service, extremely popular for its street food

⏰ 2:30 PM - VISIT: Bharat Mata Mandir (⭐ Rating: 4.2)
📍 Address: Near Civil Lines, Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh 211001
🕒 Hours: 6:00 AM - 8:00 PM
💰 Entry: INR 20-50 (approx, may vary)
⏱️ Duration: 1-2 hours
✨ Why Visit: A unique temple dedicated to Mother India, showcasing the history and culture of India through its architecture and depictions.  It's a significant nationalist landmark.

🍽️ 5:00 PM - DINNER: Kashi Chat Bhandar (⭐ Rating: 4.2)
📍 Address: 456, Chowk, Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh 211002
💰 Price Range: ₹150-300 per person
🍛 Cuisine: Street Food (Chaat)⭐ Famous For: Aloo Tikki, Dahi Puri, Pani Puri, Papdi Chaat, Kachori Sabzi
🍴 Specialties: Generous portions, fresh ingredients, and a bustling, lively atmosphere.  A Prayagraj institution.
